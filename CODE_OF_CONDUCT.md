# Code Of Conduct

## Our standard

Kosmos is committed to a respectful, constructive, and welcoming community.

Examples of positive behavior:

- being kind and clear in discussion
- assuming good intent while giving honest feedback
- focusing on the problem, not the person
- helping newer contributors get oriented

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or behavior
- bad-faith disruption of discussions or reviews
- publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate
this policy and may restrict participation when needed to protect the community.

If you experience a problem, contact the maintainers through a private channel
when possible.
